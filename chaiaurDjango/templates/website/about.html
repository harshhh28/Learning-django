About Us

Our Mission

At Chai aur Code, our mission is to bring together technology enthusiasts over a cup of chai to share knowledge, collaborate on projects, and build a strong community.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Chai aur Code started as a small meetup group in DAIICT. Over time, it has grown into a vibrant community of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts.

Meet the Team

Our team is composed of passionate individuals who are dedicated to fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment for all members.
