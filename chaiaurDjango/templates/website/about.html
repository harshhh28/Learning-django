About Chai aur Code

Our Mission

At Chai aur Code, our mission is to bring together coding enthusiasts over a cup of chai. We believe in fostering a community where learning and sharing knowledge is as refreshing as a hot cup of tea.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Chai aur Code started as a small meetup group for developers in DAIICT. Over time, it has grown into a vibrant community of coders who come together to share ideas, work on projects, and enjoy some chai.

Join Us

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, we welcome you to join our community. Come for the code, stay for the chai!
